## 1. Tong quan
Lan cap nhat nay tap trung vao hoan thien flow offline va mo rong cac module can thiet de test end-to-end.

## 2. Hang muc da hoan thanh

### 2.1 Subscription lifecycle + offline payment
- Subscription tao moi o trang thai `pending`.
- Activate member yeu cau `subscription_id` de confirm offline payment.
- Bo sung `suspend`, `resume`, `expire` cho subscription.

### 2.2 Course CRUD
- Them repo/service/handler day du cho course: create, list, get-by-id, update, delete.

### 2.3 Branch CRUD
- Them repo/service/handler day du cho branch: create, list, get-by-id, update, delete.

### 2.4 Attendance
- Them check-in va history theo subscription.
- Khi check-in `attended`/`makeup`:
  - Tao record attendance
  - Tru `remaining_sessions`
  - Cong `total_sessions_attended`
  - Tu expire subscription neu remaining = 0
